Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

The short version: LocalSub Manager does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Who We Are

LocalSub Manager is developed and published by Babu Booshik. This privacy policy explains how the LocalSub Manager mobile application ("App") handles your information.

Data We Collect

None. LocalSub Manager collects zero personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or web beacons
  • No location data
  • No device fingerprinting
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your subscription data, categories, settings, and payment history locally on your device in a private database. This data:

  • Never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it
  • Is not accessible to other apps
  • Is not backed up to our servers (we have no servers)
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App or use the "Delete All Data" feature

In-App Purchases

LocalSub Manager offers optional in-app purchases (monthly, yearly, and lifetime subscriptions) processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We use RevenueCat to manage subscription entitlements.

We do not receive or store your payment information (credit card numbers, billing addresses, etc.). Purchase transactions are handled by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat under their respective privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Data Export

You may choose to export your data as CSV or JSON using the App's export feature. Exported files are shared via your device's native share sheet and are your responsibility once exported. The App deletes temporary export files after sharing.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there is no risk of children's data being collected.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely, but we will notify users of material changes through the App.
